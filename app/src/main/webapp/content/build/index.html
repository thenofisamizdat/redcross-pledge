WE’LL THEN CARRY OUT A MATCHING PROCESS WHICH PAIRS YOUR PLEDGES WITH REFUGEE AND MIGRANT NEEDS.

Matches take into account the best interests of your community and the best interests of those you’re helping. We seek to maximise the positive impacts on both sides. When your pledge is paired, we’ll notify you and guide you through the process.

PLEDGE
Your pledges reflect what you can offer someone at any given time. If your circumstances change, you can change your pledge.
CONNECT
We’ll chat about your availability and guide you through your options before matching you to someone in need.
PAIR
Pairing is an in-depth process the Red Cross carries out on behalf of your community and we’ll figure out how what you can offer and who you can help will be of the best benefit to your community.
SUPPORT
We are on standby as your first point of contact to offer assistance whenever you, or those you are helping, need us.

THAT’S WHY THE RED CROSS EXISTS — TO HELP CITIZENS OF THE WORLD HELP OTHER CITIZENS WHO ARE SUFFERING

As people, we're not responsible for political situations like the current migration crisis. But when tragedy occurs, we want to help. We realise that it could be us and if it were, we would hope for the same support from others.
